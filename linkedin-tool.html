How to Export Your LinkedIn Data
  1. Go to LinkedIn → Settings & Privacy
  2. Click Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data
  3. Select the data categories you want (Connections, Profile, Skills, etc.)
  4. Click Request archive and wait for the email
  5. Download the ZIP file and upload it below
